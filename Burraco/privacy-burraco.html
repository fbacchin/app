Privacy Policy

Burraco Score & Burraco Light — last updated 15 July 2026

In short: the names and scores you type stay on your device — there are no accounts and no analytics. Burraco Score (paid) contains no advertising at all. Burraco Light (free) shows ads from Google AdMob and, in Europe, asks for your consent before requesting a single ad.

1. Who we are

Burraco Score and Burraco Light are iOS apps by Fabrizio Bacchin (Switzerland) that keep the score for the card game Burraco. This policy covers both apps. For anything related to it, contact bacchin@tiscali.it.

AppAdvertising
Burraco Score (paid)None. The app contains no advertising code.
Burraco Light (free)Google AdMob banner and interstitial ads — see section 4.

2. Your game data stays on your device

Player names, hands and scores you enter are held only while you play, on your device. They are never uploaded, there is no account and no cloud sync. Closing the app or starting a new game discards them.

3. Push notifications

If — and only if — you allow notifications, the app registers your device with our notification service (Back4App, a Parse hosting provider). The registration contains:

  • the anonymous Apple push token of your device (APNs)
  • a random installation identifier generated by the app
  • device type ("ios"), app version, time zone and language setting

None of this identifies you personally, it is not linked to any account and it is used exclusively to send occasional notifications about the app. It is never sold or shared for advertising. If you deny or later disable notifications, no registration is used. You may request deletion of your registration at any time by email.

4. Advertising — Burraco Light only

Burraco Light is free and paid for by advertising served by Google AdMob. To select and measure ads, Google may process data such as device and advertising identifiers, your IP address (which gives an approximate location), and information about how you interact with the ads.

Your consent (EEA, UK and Switzerland). Before the app requests any ad, it shows Google's consent message. If you consent, ads may be personalised. If you decline, the app still shows ads, but limited (non-personalised) ones. You can change your choice at any time: open the ⓘ info button in the app and tap "Impostazioni privacy".

The app does not ask for permission to track you across other companies' apps and websites (Apple's App Tracking Transparency), and therefore does not use the advertising identifier for that purpose.

How Google uses this data is described in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and in the Google Privacy Policy.

When no ad can be shown (for example with no internet connection), the app displays a promotion for other apps by the same developer. That promotion is built into the app, collects nothing and contacts no server.

5. What we don't do

  • No user accounts, no profiles
  • No analytics or usage measurement of any kind
  • No location: the app never requests it
  • No advertising whatsoever in the paid Burraco Score
  • No sale of your data
  • No data knowingly collected from children — the apps are score keepers rated 4+

6. Your rights

Under Swiss data-protection law (nFADP) and the GDPR you may request access to, correction or deletion of the data described in section 3. Since we hold no identifying information, please include the approximate date you enabled notifications so we can locate the registration. For the advertising data in section 4, Google acts as an independent controller: you can also exercise your rights directly with Google through the links above. Write to bacchin@tiscali.it.

7. Changes

If the apps gain new features (for example in-app purchases, or a request to track you across other apps), this policy will be updated before those features ship, and the "last updated" date above will change.
