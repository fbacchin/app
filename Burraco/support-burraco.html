Frequently asked questions

How do I play one against one?

Just leave the "Giocatore 2" field empty for each team and enter only the first player. The app then shows the single names instead of a pair. Fill in all four fields for a normal two-against-two game.

How many hands can I play?

As many as you need: there is no limit. You keep playing hand after hand until one team reaches the 2005-point target. The bar under each score shows how close each team is.

Who deals the cards?

Right after you confirm the names, the app asks who deals: it can draw a player at random or you can pick one yourself. From then on the deal passes automatically to the next player after every hand, alternating between the two teams. You can only change the dealer by hand before the first score is entered — once the game starts, the rotation takes over.

How do I enter a negative score?

Use the ± button in the bar above the number pad: it adds or removes the minus sign on the value you are typing. Handy for penalties, since the number pad has no minus key.

What do the numbers in each hand mean?

For every hand you see the hand total in red, with the Onori and Punti you entered below it. The red figure is the total of that hand only — the running total for the whole game is the big number at the top of the screen.

Are my scores saved when I close the app?

No. Names and scores are kept only while the game is in progress: closing the app or starting a new game with the ↻ button clears them. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Does the app have a dark mode?

Yes, and it follows your iPhone automatically: iOS Settings → Display & Brightness. There is nothing to set inside the app.

Where are the rules of the game?

Tap the ⓘ button at the top left of the app: the "Come si gioca" screen explains the target, the hands and what the screen shows. The app version and build number are at the bottom of that screen — useful if you report a problem.

Burraco Light: I see a promo for other apps instead of an advert

That happens when no advert can be served — typically with no internet connection. Rather than leaving an empty gap, the app shows one of our other apps. As soon as a real advert is available it takes its place.

Burraco Light: how do I change my advertising privacy choice?

Tap the ⓘ button and then "Impostazioni privacy" in the "Come si gioca" screen. The option appears where European rules require it. See the Privacy Policy for the details.

I don't receive notifications

Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Burraco and make sure they are allowed. Notifications are optional and only used for occasional news about the app.

Reporting a problem

To help us reproduce it quickly, please include your iPhone model, the iOS version and the app version and build (shown at the bottom of the "Come si gioca" screen), plus what you did just before the problem appeared.
