Privacy Policy

Gottardo Live — last updated 12 July 2026

In short: Gottardo Live has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising and no tracking. The only data that ever leaves your device is an anonymous push-notification token — and only if you choose to enable notifications.

1. Who we are

Gottardo Live is an iOS app by Fabrizio Bacchin (Switzerland) that shows real-time traffic information for the Gotthard road tunnel. For anything related to this policy, contact bacchin@tiscali.it.

2. Data the app sends (push notifications only)

If — and only if — you allow notifications, the app registers your device with our notification service (Back4App, a Parse hosting provider). The registration contains:

  • the anonymous Apple push token of your device (APNs)
  • a random installation identifier generated by the app
  • device type ("ios"), app version, time zone and language setting

None of this identifies you personally, it is not linked to any account and it is used exclusively to deliver traffic notifications. It is never sold or shared for advertising. If you deny or later disable notifications, no registration is created or used. You may request deletion of your registration at any time by email.

3. Services the app talks to

To display live data, the app downloads content from the following services. As with any internet request, these services technically process your IP address for the duration of the request; the app adds no identifiers of its own.

opentransportdata.swiss (Swiss Confederation / FEDRO)official queue lengths, waiting times and traffic alerts

4. Data stored on your device only

Your settings and the local traffic-history samples used for the 24-hour chart are stored on your device and never uploaded. Deleting the app deletes them.

5. What we don't do

  • No user accounts, no profiles
  • No analytics or usage measurement of any kind
  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers
  • No tracking across apps or websites, no sale of data
  • No data knowingly collected from children — the app is a traffic utility rated 4+

6. Your rights

Under Swiss data-protection law (nFADP) and the GDPR you may request access to, correction or deletion of the data described in section 2. Since we hold no identifying information, please include the approximate date you enabled notifications so we can locate the registration. Write to bacchin@tiscali.it.

7. Changes

If the app gains new features (for example advertising or in-app purchases), this policy will be updated before those features ship, and the "last updated" date above will change.
