Gottardo Live — last updated 22 July 2026

1. Who we are

Gottardo Live is an iOS app by Fabrizio Bacchin (Switzerland) that shows real-time traffic information for the Gotthard road tunnel. For anything related to this policy, contact bacchin@tiscali.it.

2. Advertising

The free app shows one banner advert supplied by Google AdMob. To select, deliver, measure and protect those ads, Google's advertising SDK may process technical data from your device — such as a device/advertising identifier, IP address, coarse (non-precise) location and ad-interaction data. For this processing Google acts as an independent data controller under its own policies.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland we ask for your consent through Google's consent form before any ad is requested. You can change or withdraw that choice at any time in Settings → Privacy options inside the app. If you decline, only non-personalised (“limited”) ads are shown.

The app does not use App Tracking Transparency and does not read Apple's advertising identifier (IDFA) for cross-app or cross-site tracking. For privacy-preserving install measurement it relies on Apple's SKAdNetwork. You can remove advertising entirely — see the next section.

3. In-app purchases

You can remove the advert with an in-app purchase: a one-month option (which does not renew automatically) or a one-time permanent purchase. Payments are processed by Apple; we never receive or store your payment details. Whether you have an active purchase, and its expiry date for the monthly option, is kept only on your device. Subscriptions and purchases are managed through your Apple Account.

4. Data the app sends (push notifications)

If — and only if — you allow notifications, the app registers your device with our notification service (Back4App, a Parse hosting provider). The registration contains:

  • the anonymous Apple push token of your device (APNs)
  • a random installation identifier generated by the app
  • device type ("ios"), app version, time zone and language setting

None of this identifies you personally, it is not linked to any account and it is used exclusively to deliver traffic notifications. It is never sold or shared for advertising. If you deny or later disable notifications, no registration is created or used. You may request deletion of your registration at any time by email.

5. Services the app talks to

To display live data and ads, the app connects to the services below. As with any internet request, they technically process your IP address for the duration of the request.

6. Data stored on your device only

Your settings, your ad-consent choice, your purchase status and the local traffic-history samples used for the 24-hour chart are stored on your device and are not uploaded to us. Deleting the app deletes them.

7. What we don't do

  • No user accounts, no profiles
  • No analytics or usage measurement of our own
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking, and no use of the IDFA
  • No sale of your data
  • We never receive your payment information
  • No data knowingly collected from children — the app is a traffic utility rated 4+

8. Your rights

Under Swiss data-protection law (nFADP) and the GDPR you may request access to, correction or deletion of the data described in section 4. Since we hold no identifying information, please include the approximate date you enabled notifications so we can locate the registration. Write to bacchin@tiscali.it. For the advertising data processed by Google, please refer to Google and Google's own privacy controls.

9. Changes

This policy will be updated whenever the app's data practices change, and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
